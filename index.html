Trend of Hospital Visits

Trend of Hospital Visits

Overall, the data shows an increase in total hospital visits from 2011 to 2013 in all subcounties. Gatanga had the highest increase, with total hospital visits increasing by 78485 from 2011 to 2013. Kandara had the second highest increase, with total hospital visits increasing by 62565 from 2011 to 2013. Kiharu had the third highest increase, with total hospital visits increasing by 87384 from 2011 to 2013.

In terms of percentage increase, Kiharu had the highest percentage increase in total hospital visits from 2011 to 2013, with a 328.6% increase. Gatanga had the second highest percentage increase, with a 142.6% increase. Kandara had the third highest percentage increase, with a 138.2% increase.

Facility Distribution in Murang'a in 2013

There is an uneven distribution of healthcare facilities in Muranga County, with some sub-counties having significantly more facilities than others. Gatanga has the most facilities with 27, followed by Kandara with 22 and Kangema with 18. Kigumo and Kiharu both have 15 facilities, while Mathioya and Muranga South have the fewest with 11 and 15 facilities respectively. It is important for there to be a balanced distribution of healthcare facilities in a region to ensure that all residents have access to quality healthcare. It may be necessary to consider expanding or building new facilities in sub-counties with fewer resources in order to achieve universal health coverage in Muranga County.
