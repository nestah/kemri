Analysis based on Functionality

Here, "Functionality" is defined as whether the facilities are offering the basic minimum health services as prescribed by national GoK policy & guidelines for various categories of health facilities.

There is no facility in Kigumo and Mathioya that have Good functionality.

In terms of the number of healthcare facilities in each functional category, Gatanga has the most healthcare facilities classified as being in good functional condition, with 2 facilities. Kandara and Kiharu have the most healthcare facilities classified as being in below functional condition, with 7 facilities each. Kiharu also has the most healthcare facilities classified as being in fair functional condition, with 12 facilities. Kandara has the most healthcare facilities classified as being in average functional condition, with 12 facilities.

Sum of functionality based on levels

In terms of the types of facilities, there are 103 dispensaries, 24 health centers, and 5 sub-district hospitals. The majority of the facilities classified as being in "below" functionality are dispensaries (41 out of 48), while the majority of the facilities classified as being in "good" functionality are sub-district hospitals (5 out of 10).

Overall, these results suggest that there may be a need to prioritize upgrading or improving the functionality of the dispensaries in the sub-counties, as a larger proportion of these facilities are classified as being in "below" functionality compared to the other types of facilities. It may also be worth considering why the sub-district hospitals have a higher percentage of "good" functionality and whether these practices can be implemented in other types of facilities to improve their functionality.
