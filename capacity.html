Analysis of Capacity

Majority of healthcare facilities in the sub-counties have "minimum capacity," with 98 out of 132 facilities falling into this category. A smaller number of facilities have "no capacity" (27 out of 132), while an even smaller number have "sufficient installed capacity" (7 out of 132).

In terms of the specific sub-counties, Gatanga has the highest number of facilities with "minimum capacity" (17 out of 23), followed by Kandara (21 out of 22) and Kiharu (22 out of 27). Mathioya has the highest percentage of facilities with "no capacity," with 5 out of 12 facilities falling into this category.
