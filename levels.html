Comparison of Number of levels of Hospitals per Sub-county

Gatanga has the most facilities, with 17 dispensaries, 5 health centers, and 1 sub-district hospital. Kandara and Kiharu have the second most facilities, with 21 dispensaries and 19 dispensaries, respectively. Kangema, Kigumo, and Muthioya have a moderate number of facilities, with 14, 11, and 8 dispensaries, respectively. Muranga south has the least number of facilities, with only 12 dispensaries.

Dispensary Visits per sub-county

Sub-counties with a higher number of dispensaries tend to have a higher number of visits to healthcare facilities. For example, Gatanga, Kandara, and Kiharu, which have 17, 21, and 19 dispensaries, respectively, also have the highest number of visits to healthcare facilities in 2011, 2012, and 2013.

On the other hand, sub-counties with a lower number of dispensaries tend to have a lower number of visits to healthcare facilities. For example, Muthioya, which has only 8 dispensaries, also has the lowest number of visits to healthcare facilities in 2011, 2012, and 2013. Muranga south, which has 12 dispensaries, also has a lower number of visits compared to the other sub-counties.

Health Center Visits per sub-county

It is worth noting that Kandara does not have any health centers and also has a very low number of visits to healthcare facilities in 2011, 2012, and 2013. This may suggest that the availability of health centers is an important factor in determining the number of visits to healthcare facilities in each sub-county.

Sub-district facility Visits per sub-county

Gatanga, Kandara, Kangema, Kiharu, and Muranga south, which all have a sub-district hospital, also have relatively high numbers of visits to healthcare facilities in 2011, 2012, and 2013.

On the other hand, sub-counties without a sub-district hospital tend to have a lower number of visits to healthcare facilities. For example, Kigumo and Muthioya, which do not have a sub-district hospital, also have a very low number of visits to healthcare facilities in 2011, 2012, and 2013.

It is worth noting that the number of visits to healthcare facilities in sub-counties with a sub-district hospital is not consistently high across all years. For example, Gatanga and Kandara have relatively high numbers of visits in 2011 and 2012, but a lower number of visits in 2013. Similarly, Kangema and Kiharu have relatively high numbers of visits in 2011 and 2013, but a lower number of visits in 2012.
